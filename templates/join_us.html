JOIN US

Fill out the form below and one of our recruiters will get in touch with you shortly.

How to fill out the form.

If you're interested in joining His Majesty's Armed Forces, you can fill out the form below and we can guide you on how to prepare to join.

  • Enter your full name
  • Enter your email or phone number so that we can easily get in touch with you
  • Specify if you wanted to be an Officer, Enlisted soldier, or a specialist. See the section of the website that define those.
  • Enter anything else that you want us to know about you or anything that you want to know from us before we can keep in touch

Our recruiters are here to help guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have. We look forward to hearing from you!

Approaching your Recruiters

If you're interested in joining His Majesty's Armed Forces, there are a few things you should know when approaching our recruiters:

  • Be prepared to talk about the reasons why you want to join
  • Be prepared to talk about your background, education, and experience
  • Be honest about any past legal or medical issues that may affect your eligibility to join
  • Be willing to undergo physical and mental evaluations as part of the recruitment process
  • Ask about the kind of jobs that we offer
  • Ask about the recruitment process and the requirements to join
  • Ask questions and seek clarification on anything you're unsure of

Our recruiters are here to help guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have. We look forward to hearing from you!
