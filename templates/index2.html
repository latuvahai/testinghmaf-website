HIS MAJESTY'S ARMED FORCES

TERRA MARIQUE

Welcome to the official website of His Majesty's Armed Forces of the Kingdom of Tonga. The Mission of His Majesty's Armed Forces is to defend the Kingdom against external threats, provide protection for our sovereignty, including our territorial waters, and support civil authorities in creating a secure and peaceful environment to enhance the social, economic, and political development of the Kingdom.

Becoming an enlisted soldier/sailor or a Commissioned Officer

Enlisted Soldiers/Sailors are the backbone of the Force, responsible for carrying out orders and ensuring the success of each mission. Commissioned Officers are important leaders in the Force, tasked with making critical decisions and being responsible for the safety of Soldiers/Sailors. Depending on the path you take, there are a few things you must do to get started.
