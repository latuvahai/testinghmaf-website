Our Services

HMAF consists of several units responsible for defending the country's borders, conducting operations at sea, providing logistical support, and protecting the monarchy and people of Tonga

Learn more

Land Force

The Land Force is responsible for ground-based military operations. It consists of soldiers who are trained to use a variety of weapons and equipment to defend the nation's interests and support national security objectives. The Land Force plays a crucial role in defending the country's borders, assisting civil authority in local matters, and providing humanitarian assistance and disaster relief in times of crisis.

Learn more

Royal Navy

The Navy is responsible for conducting maritime operations, including both defensive and offensive operations at sea. It consists of sailors who operate patrol boats, landing crafts and small boats to protect the country's interests. The Navy's mission includes maintaining freedom of the seas, safeguarding shipping lanes, and providing humanitarian assistance and disaster relief in coastal areas.

Learn more

Royal Guards

The Royal Guards are responsible for protecting the monarch and other members of the royal family. They are typically highly trained and specialized soldiers who are entrusted with safeguarding the royal family and their residences, as well as important national landmarks and institutions. In addition to their protective duties, the Royal Guards also participate in ceremonial events and perform other tasks as directed by the King.

Learn more

Support

The Support Unit provide logistical support to HMAF operations by providing supplies, equipment, and services to the organizations. It encompasses everything from transportation and storage to maintenance and repair, and ensures that troops have the resources they need to accomplish their missions.

Learn more
