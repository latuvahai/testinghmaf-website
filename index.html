HIS MAJESTY'S ARMED FORCES

TERRA MARIQUE

Welcome to the official website of His Majesty's Armed Forces of the Kingdom of Tonga. The Mission of His Majesty's Armed Forces is to defend the Kingdom against external threats, provide protection for our sovereignty, including our territorial waters, and support civil authorities in creating a secure and peaceful environment to enhance the social, economic, and political development of the Kingdom.
